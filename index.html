What we do

Commemorative
stone for the
moments that last.

A building opening, a Royal visit, a company anniversary, a foundation stone — these are one-off moments. The stone that marks them is permanent. We treat the making accordingly.

We produce commemorative plaques, unveiling stones, foundation stones and dedication pieces for corporate communications teams, local authorities, institutions, charities and event planners.

Our standard lead time is six weeks from signed artwork approval. We can work faster. We also hire the easel and co-ordinate the ceremony logistics — if you want a single point of contact for the whole event.

Services

What we make.

01

Commemorative Plaques

Hand-cut or photo-blasted commemorative plaques in Welsh slate, Portland limestone, granite and marble. Gilded lettering standard on request. Wall-mounted or freestanding.

02

Foundation Stones

Foundation and cornerstone pieces for new buildings and major developments. Inscription-led design. Material chosen to complement the building's own palette. In-situ installation.

03

Royal & State Unveilings

Plaques for official ceremonies involving Royal or ministerial unveiling. We have produced for King Charles III and for both the Welsh and Scottish Governments. We understand the protocol.

04

Easel Hire & Staging

Our purpose-built ceremonial easel for plaque unveilings. Available nationwide. We co-ordinate delivery, setup, collection and can manage the ceremony logistics as turnkey service.

6 weeks

Standard lead time

From signed artwork approval to completed piece. Rush production available where the ceremony date requires it.

UK-wide

Delivery & install

We deliver and install anywhere in the UK. International shipping available. Site survey co-ordinated in advance.

Royal grade

Quality standard

Every piece is produced to the same standard — whether it's going into a boardroom or being unveiled by a Head of State.
