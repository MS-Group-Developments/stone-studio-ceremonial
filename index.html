Ceremonial

Marking a moment permanently.

Foundation stones, commemorative plaques, civic ceremony pieces and opening markers. Work made for occasions that should never be forgotten.

We work with local authorities, institutions, developers and private clients. From a simple commemorative plaque to a full ceremonial stone with time capsule and unveiling provision.

Services

What we make

Foundation stones

Laid-in foundation stones with carved inscription for buildings, bridges and civic infrastructure.

Opening & unveiling plaques

Wall-mounted and freestanding plaques for official openings, royal visits and civic ceremonies.

Commemorative panels

Heritage panels, centenary stones and anniversary markers for institutions, estates and public buildings.

Time capsule provision

Stones designed to receive a time capsule cavity. We co-ordinate the cavity, seal and inscription.

Heritage plaques

Period-appropriate stonework for listed buildings, conservation areas and historic estates.

Dedication stones

Named dedication stones for parks, gardens, public spaces and memorial gardens.

As part of the service

Easel hire & presentation.

Presenting your plaque on a beautifully crafted hardwood easel, complete with hand-made curtains and drapes, means your unveiling can take place wherever you choose. We manage every element from concept through to collection.

Concept & design

We advise on the right easel size and configuration to suit the occasion, the plaque and the venue.

Sourcing & draping

Hand-made curtains and drapes in gold, crimson, purple, emerald and blue — sourced and fitted to the easel to match your ceremony colours and occasion.

Delivery

The easel is delivered by us ready for easy assembly, with easy downloadable setup instructions available for your team.

Ceremony

The plaque is delivered with the easel, mounted and ready for your prestigious event — nothing left to arrange on the day.

Collection

Once the ceremony is complete, we arrange collection of the easel — leaving nothing for you to organise after the event.

Sustainably Sourced

British-quarried natural stone contributes up to 304% less carbon than imported alternatives — we work exclusively with UK quarries.
